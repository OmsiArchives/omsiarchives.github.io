Vehicle Archives - Senior LO-916




This bus is based off of a real Senior LO-916 buses manufactured by Marcopolo, a brazilian company who manufactures buses.

This bus was released in 29th of Febuary, 2014, and published on Frank Magalhães personal site for mods, it was originally paid, but people started leaking the mod so people could enjoy without paying it. then Frank, creator of the mod, made it public sometime in 2016 as he was aware of this situation. The bus was popular back then as it was detailed like any other FF Design projects, its an edited Senior G7 Volkswagen made by a group known as "OMSI BUS BR" before "FF Design" was named. Their first mod, Senior G7 Volksbus was finalized in November 2nd, 2013, and the bus was made on ZModeler in 2012, later adapted for OMSI sometime in 2013-2014.
