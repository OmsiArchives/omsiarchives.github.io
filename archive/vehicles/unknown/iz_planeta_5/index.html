Vehicle Archives - IZH Planeta 5


IZH Planeta was a series of Russian motorcycles manufactured from 1962 until 2008. The mod is based off it.

This bus was released in 28th of Febuary, 2012, according to the Mediafire upload and possibly published on ofpmafia.eu under the motorcycles section. it is extremely buggy, especially the driver's seat placement and floating tickets. The collision seems to be ripped from MAN SD202, so its reccomended to disable AI and Ground collions in the menu.
